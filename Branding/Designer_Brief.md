# VanGo — Brand Illustration Brief

## The brand in one sentence
VanGo is a mobile app for finding food trucks. The name is a play on **Van** (the food truck), **Go** (action), and **Van Gogh** — the painter. The brand identity leans into the Van Gogh association: post-Impressionist warmth, painterly confidence, food-as-art.

## What we need
Three illustrations, post-Impressionist oil-painted style, tuned for use in an iOS app:

### 1. App Icon — primary deliverable
- **Final size:** 1024 × 1024 PNG, **no transparency, no rounded corners** (Apple applies the squircle mask)
- **Concept:** A bold "VG" wordmark in sunflower yellow, hand-painted with visible impasto brushstrokes, centered on a swirling cobalt-blue night sky in the manner of Van Gogh's *Starry Night*. Glowing yellow stars with soft halos. Visible canvas texture.
- **Must read at 60 × 60 px** — that's iOS's smallest icon size. Test by viewing the final image at 60px and confirming the "VG" is still legible.
- Source files: layered PSD or AI welcome.

### 2. Brushstroke Van Mark — illustrative secondary
- **Final size:** SVG (vector preferred) or 2048 × 1280 PNG with transparent background
- **Concept:** A side-view of a yellow food truck van rendered in Van Gogh's brushstroke style. Thick impasto, expressive, post-Impressionist. Cobalt-blue window with a Starry Night spiral inside the window. Terracotta motion lines streaking behind suggesting motion. Black wheels with yellow centers. Used in the splash screen and About screen — should feel like a painting, not a clip-art icon.

### 3. Hero Illustration — for the About screen and marketing
- **Final size:** 2880 × 1620 PNG (or vector)
- **Concept:** A wider scene: yellow food truck under a swirling Starry Night sky, sunflowers beside it, warm yellow light spilling from the serving window, cobblestone street. Painterly, atmospheric, narrative. This is the brand's "moment" image.

## Style references
- Vincent van Gogh, *Starry Night over the Rhône* (1888) — for the swirling sky
- Vincent van Gogh, *Sunflowers* (1888) — for the chrome yellow palette
- Vincent van Gogh, *Café Terrace at Night* (1888) — for warm yellow light against cobalt sky (this is essentially "food truck at night" aesthetic, perfect reference)
- Vincent van Gogh, *The Yellow House* (1888) — for the sun-baked yellow-on-cobalt feel

## Technical requirements
- Editable source files (Procreate, PSD, AI, or Figma) — we want to make tweaks later.
- The app icon **must** be flat PNG with no alpha channel (App Store requires this).
- Provide 1×, 2×, 3× rasters for the brushstroke van mark (or just SVG if your style is vector-friendly).
- Color values must match the hex codes in the palette table — measure with a color picker on delivery.

## What to avoid
- **Generic/AI-flavoured aesthetics**: bland gradients, perfect symmetry, copy-paste sunflowers. We want something that feels hand-painted and confident.
- **Too literal Van Gogh**: don't try to fool anyone into thinking it's a real Van Gogh painting. The reference is post-Impressionist *style* and palette, applied to modern subject matter (a food truck app).
- **Cute / cartoon van**: keep the van confident and grown-up. No googly eyes, no smiling truck.
- **Over-busy composition**: each illustration should have one clear focal point.
