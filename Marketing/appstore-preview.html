Description

Craving something delicious? VanGo connects you with the best food trucks in your area — in real time.

Whether you're hunting for authentic street tacos, slow-smoked BBQ, wood-fired pizza, or vegan comfort food, VanGo makes it easy to discover, track, and navigate to food trucks near you.
Craving something delicious? VanGo connects you with the best food trucks in your area — in real time.

Whether you're hunting for authentic street tacos, slow-smoked BBQ, wood-fired pizza, or vegan comfort food, VanGo makes it easy to discover, track, and navigate to food trucks near you.

FOR FOOD LOVERS:
• Search by cuisine — Mexican, BBQ, Korean, Italian, Seafood, Vegan, and more
• See live locations on the map with real-time GPS tracking
• Browse full menus with prices before you go
• Read reviews from other food truck fans
• Get one-tap driving directions via Apple Maps
• Score exclusive deals and coupons from your favorite trucks
• See which trucks are open right now with live status updates

FOR FOOD TRUCK OWNERS:
VanGo is also built for you. List your truck, reach new customers, and grow your business — all from your phone.

• Create your truck profile with menu, photos, hours, and social links
• Pin your location or broadcast your live GPS position
• Toggle your "Open for Business" status so customers know when you're serving
• Create and manage coupons to drive traffic
• Run targeted ad campaigns to boost your visibility
• Choose from flexible subscription tiers that fit your budget

TWO SIMPLE PLANS FOR TRUCK OWNERS:
• Basic ($9.99/mo) — Get listed, post your menu, collect reviews, pin your location
• Pro ($24.99/mo) — Everything in Basic plus live GPS tracking, "Open" status broadcasting, and priority placement in search results

VanGo is where street food meets smart technology. Time to VanGo — find your next favorite meal on wheels.

App Review Notes

VanGo is a dual-sided marketplace for food truck discovery. Users can browse as "Food Lovers" (consumers) or "Truck Owners" (business). The app uses location services to show nearby food trucks on a map. Truck owners can subscribe via in-app purchases (Basic: $9.99/mo, Pro: $24.99/mo). Coupon redemptions include a 15% revenue share to the platform.
