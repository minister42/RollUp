What VanGo gives you

📍
Live location, broadcast once

Tap "I'm Open" — your pin lights up on the map for every nearby user. No more 8 Instagram stories a day.

🍴
Your full menu, always current

Photos, prices, dietary tags. Update once, customers see it instantly. Replaces your menu PDF, your Yelp listing, and that whiteboard.

Slow Tuesday? Push a 25%-off coupon at 11:30am. Customers within 1 mile get pinged. Track redemptions in real time.

Built-in ratings & reviews. Social proof for first-timers without you chasing Yelp.
